Purpose & Overview

The Intramurals 2026 is the premier annual sporting event designed to foster camaraderie, sportsmanship, and healthy competition among students across all departments. This year's theme, "Breaking Barriers, Building Champions," emphasizes personal growth and team unity.

Our platform serves as the central hub for real-time statistics, match schedules, and official standings, ensuring transparency and engagement for every participant and spectator.

Rules & Scoring System

Overall Point Weights

  • Gold Medal 5 Points
  • Silver Medal 3 Points
  • Bronze Medal 1 Point

Match Points (League)

  • Win +3 Points
  • Draw +1 Point
  • Loss 0 Points

"In case of a tie in total points, the team with the higher number of Gold medals takes the lead."
